Why Xeroderma Pigmentosum Patients ?

There are 1000 "children of the moon" in Tunisia. Relative to a population of 11 million, this proportion is arguably the highest percentage worldwide. By way of comparison, France has 65 cases out of 65 million inhabitants, the United States has 300 cases for a population of 320 million souls. Moon children suffer from an incurable genetic disease scientifically called Xeroderma Pigmentosum. They are exposed from a young age to multiple risks including skin cancer and blindness because of the devastating effect of ultraviolet rays on their body.
